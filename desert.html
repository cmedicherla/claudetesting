What is a Stroke Desert?

A stroke desert is a region where residents are located more than 1 hour travel time (approximately 60 miles) from the nearest comprehensive stroke center. The first hour after stroke onset is critical for treatment - areas beyond this "golden window" face significant barriers to accessing hyperacute stroke care.
